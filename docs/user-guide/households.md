# Managing Households

A household is the top-level organizational unit in FreezerMan. It represents your home (or any group of people who share freezers). All freezers, compartments, and items belong to a household, and all members of a household can view and manage its inventory.

## Creating a Household

After registering your account, create a household to get started. Give it a name that makes sense for your situation -- for example, "Home" or "Lake House".

When you create a household, you automatically become its **owner**.

## Switching Between Households

If you belong to more than one household (for example, your own home and a family member's), you can switch between them from the main navigation. Your current household determines which freezers and items you see.

## Roles

Every household member has one of two roles:

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full control -- manage members, transfer ownership, delete the household, plus everything a member can do |
| **Member** | Add and edit freezers, compartments, and items |

There is exactly one owner per household. All other users are members.

### What Owners Can Do

- Invite new members (via QR code or invite code)
- Remove members from the household
- Transfer ownership to another member
- Delete the household entirely
- Everything a member can do

### What Members Can Do

- Add, edit, and archive freezers, compartments, and items
- View all household data
- View other members

## Transferring Ownership

If you're the owner and want to hand control to someone else, you can transfer ownership to any current member. Once transferred:

- The other member becomes the new owner
- You become a regular member
- This action takes effect immediately

> **Note:** Ownership transfer cannot be undone by the previous owner. The new owner would need to transfer it back.

## Removing Members

Only the owner can remove members from a household. Removing a member revokes their access to all household data immediately. Items they previously added remain in the inventory -- they are not deleted.

## Deleting a Household

Only the owner can delete a household.

> **Warning:** Deleting a household permanently removes **all** of its data -- every freezer, compartment, item, and change history record. This action cannot be undone. Make sure you truly want to delete everything before confirming.

All members lose access immediately when a household is deleted.
